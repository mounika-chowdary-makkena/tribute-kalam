Tribute to A.P.J Abdul kalam


About A.P.J Abdul kalam

A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, the "Missile Man of India," was born on October 15, 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. He served as the 11th President of India and was a renowned scientist, known for his work in India's space and missile program

ACHIEVEMENTS

  • Developed India's first satellite launch vehicle (SLV-III)
  • Led India's successful nuclear tests in 1998
  • Wrote books like "Wings of Fire" and "India 2020"
  • Received the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian award
